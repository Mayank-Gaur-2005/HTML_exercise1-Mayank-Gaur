Pollution of Yamuna river

High levels of ammonia in the Yamuna indicate the presence of untreated industrial chemicals and sewage, which have polluted the raw water supply chain. This pollution has caused problems for water treatment plants and the production of drinking water.8 Feb 2024. Untreated wastewater and poor quality of water discharged from the wastewater treatment plants are the major reasons of Yamuna's pollution in Delhi.Delhi Jal Board reported a high level of pollutants in the Yamuna river at the Wazirabad pond which has led to a reduction in water production by 25-30 per cent at the Wazirabad and Chandrawal treatment plants.26 Dec 2023
